Project Purpose

Anime Lens is a web application designed to help users discover new anime and manga titles tailored to their preferences. With thousands of titles available, this site offers a more personalized experience than traditional recommendation platforms. It brings together discovery tools, user-generated content, and tracking features in one place.

On this platform, users can expect a modern, easy-to-use interface that helps them explore popular and trending titles, keep track of their watchlists, and interact with the anime community. The project integrates multiple anime APIs to deliver accurate, relevant, and engaging results.

Purpose of Anime Lens

Anime Lens is your personalized gateway into the world of anime and manga. With thousands of titles available, it can be overwhelming to decide what to watch or read next. This platform helps users discover new series based on their interests, search for anime titles, track their watchlist, and connect with the anime community through discussions and reviews.

How to Use This Website

  1. Discover Page: Use the search bar to find anime by name or keyword. You can view title details and recommendations powered by anime APIs.
  2. Watchlist: Save your favorite or to-watch anime titles. This helps you keep track of shows you’re interested in.
  3. Community: Read and post comments about anime series. Share your thoughts, get suggestions, and connect with fellow fans.
  4. Trending Section: Check out what’s hot right now with the trending anime slider on the Home page.

Whether you're a seasoned watcher or just starting your anime journey, Anime Lens makes it easier to explore, organize, and enjoy your anime experience.
